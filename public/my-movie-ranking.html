The Top Three Christopher Nolan Movies

My personal list of Christopher Nolan's best movies

Interstellar

Is there even a question anymore? From the music, to the cinematography to the acting, everything about this film is exemplery

Oppenheimer

Controversial choice, but I think this is comes in second, not only because of the awesome performance of a stellar cast, but also because of how well this movie was made and how it amazingly it delivers its message

The Batman Trilogy

Yes, this is cheating, because there are technically three movies, with The Dark Knight being the best one, however from the narrative perspective, we can view all three films on a continuum, showing a whole story over three movies. If we look at it like that, these iconic movies are what put Nolan on the front page, and what can I say about everyrhing from the action, the music, to the acting? Just spectacular.
